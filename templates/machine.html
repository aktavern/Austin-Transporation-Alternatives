Machine Learning Overview

An initial linear regression scored very weakly. Linear regressions continued to not perform well, even with some feature engineering. Other linear regression models, such as Lasso, continued to not perform well on this dataset. Finally, a decision tree regression with further feature engineering was able to predict ranges of demand for a particular device with 90% accuracy!

I was able to find relationships between the hour, average temperature, day of week, vehicle type, month, and average humidity on raw count data. However, this model only had an adjusted R-squared of 0.549, and the testing score only achieved 0.39. I then binned the hours and months. After some experimenting, grouping by 4 hour intervals and 3 month intervals was best combination.
