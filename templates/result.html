The model uses the following variables to predict demand for scooters or bicycles:

  • Day
  • Day of Week
  • Month
  • Year
  • Hour
  • Average Temperature
  • Average Humidity
  • Average Wind Speed
  • Precipitation
  • The type of vehicle
  • Hour classified into one of 6 groups
  • Month classified into one of in 4 groups
    • If a user inputs a date in the future, the app queries the backend database for a historical average of the temperature, humidity, wind speed, and precipitation for that date. It then merges those averages back into the user-provided variables to predict demand for that vehicle within those parameters.
