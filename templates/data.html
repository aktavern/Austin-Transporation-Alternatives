Data Overview

The initial dataset included information on trips made on both bicycles and scooters between 2018-01-01 and 2019-10-10. Analysis found that bicycles are used to travel greater distances and for greater lengths than scooters. Customers are more likely to use scooters on the weekend, and use bicycles on a more steady basis. Scooter usage is highest between noon and 5pm while bike usage remains fairly constant between 8am and 5pm. Usage for both vehicles increases in March and September.

The original dataset was combined with daily historical weather from wunderground.com. The weather data was then evaluated against overall demand for both bicycles and scooters overall. Initial analysis shows that weather did not have a significant impact on scooter and bicycle demand. However, it does play a role in predicting demand for both vehicles.

Average Trip Distance/Trip Duration by Vehicle

Note: The trip distance is measured in meters, while the trip duration is measured in seconds.
Customers are using bicycles to travel greater distances and for longer durations, while scooters are being used for shorter and more brief trips. This could be due to the fact that scooters have two critical limitations: first, many companies enforce geoboundaries so that scooters stay within the same regions when they are operable; second, the city is fundamentally divided into sections with its highways, which cannot be accessed via scooters. One other explanation is that scooters are a new phenomenon within the city, and many of the trips may be users briefly using them for the experience during a free trial service. In sum, the selection of a bike or scooter will depend on the need of the customer, and the restrictions of the device. In addition, the selection of a brand may depend on the needs of the customer as well, as each brand has different areas of operation.

Usage by Day of Week

Note: The days of week correspond where Sunday = 0, and so on.
Scooter users are much more likely to use the devices Thursday through Saturday. This implies that the average customer is using these devices for brief outings and not as an extension of their daily routine, such as getting from a bus stop to work. Meanwhile, bicycle usage remains low, but consistent throughout the entire week. Given that the average distance and duration of bicycle usage is higher than scooters, this could imply that some customers use bicycles as an extension of their daily routine, although that customer base remains very small.

Usage by Hour

Scooter usage is highest between noon and 5pm, and is lowest between 3am and 6am. This further confirms that scooters are not being used for daily routines and are instead being used for short trips at an irregular basis. Bicycles are used somewhat gradually between 8am and 5pm, which confirms that they may be extensions of daily routines. One data point which would help with this analysis but is not available in the dataset is the start and end locations of scooters and bicycles. Given the location of where users are beginning and ending, further assumptions could be drawn from hourly usage as to whether users are going to work or school, or are using the devices on an adhoc basis.

Monthly Usage

Usage for both scooters dramatically increases during March and September. SXSW is during the month of March and is hosted in areas where all scooter operators are available. Both devices may be extensions of this conference. Overall usage of scooters is highest in September, which is when colleges begin their sessions fully. The decrease following September may be due to fewer new college students using the devices. Demand overall decreases between November - January, and between April - June. This may also be tied back to college sessions, as semesters typically end during those months, and many students may be returning to their home towns during that time.

The scatter plots shown indicate that while there may be increased demand under certain conditions, demand can also be low under the same conditions. For example, demand can be high or low when the average temperature is between about 65 and 75 degrees.
